Since Animal Crossing: Pocket Camp's release, players have attempted to keep track of the essences and materials that villagers hand out to help them complete their goals. Infographic makers, such as reddit users SenorDeeebs and kimvoslice (whose design is currently updated by GrokNotMock), have utilized visual elements in assisting players with their endeavors.

This project automates the infographic process, using AngularJS and Python scripting to update table contents. This procedure will also extendable for future analysis of animal demographics and Nintendo updates that may include new essences and materials.

Information and villager/material icons were compiled from the Animal Crossing Wiki. Essence icons and background art were hand-recreated from Animal Crossing Pocket Camp.
